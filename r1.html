PREPARATION OF IDLI



Idli is a soft, pillowy steamed savory cake made from fermented rice and lentil batter. The lentils used in making the idli batter are urad dal (hulled black gram). Black gram is also known as matpe beans, urad beans. To make idli the off white colored husked/hulled black gram is used â€“ it can be split or whole.
Alternative names Idly, iddali
Course Breakfast, dinner
Place of origin India
Region or state South India
Associated cuisine India, Sri Lanka
Serving temperature Hot with a condiment such as sambar or chutney in South India, ghugni and aloo dum in East India
Main ingredients Black lentils (de-husked), rice
Variations Button idli, tatte idli, sanna, sambar idli, rava idli, masala idli, shell idli
